Edge Detection

Edge detection can be done in different ways, all with linear algebra at their core. As you know, images can be represented with matrices. These matrices can then be manipulated to do cool things such as edge detection. First, we will introduce a new matrix operation called kernel convolution. Never heard of that? Think it sounds like corn? Are you getting hungry for popcorn? Well, there's no popcorn, but it does involve taking a matrix called a kernel and traversing anothr matrix, taking kernels out of that.
